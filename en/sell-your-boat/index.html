How we work

What to expect when selling with SentYacht

Well-positioned pricing

An unfounded high price drives buyers away. A low one costs you money. We analyse recent comparables to find the right range.

Presentation that sells

A good boat poorly presented loses opportunities. Professional photography, detailed technical sheet and description — no exaggeration, no omission.

Screened buyers

We don't arrange viewings with browsers. Every contact is screened before they step aboard your boat.

One contact, not a department

One person manages your sale from start to finish. You always know what's happening and who to call.

Full discretion if you need it

No online listing, no portals, no exposure. Only pre-selected buyers that you authorise.

Mediterranean market, direct knowledge

From El Masnou we operate across Maresme, Barcelona, Costa Brava and the Balearics. We know the ports, the buyers and the real prices.

Valuation based on real data

We don't offer guesswork. We analyse recent transactions of comparable vessels, assess overall condition, maintenance history, refits and current market dynamics. The result is a well-founded price range that lets you decide with information, not expectations.

Presentation that builds buyer confidence

A well-presented boat sells faster and better. We carry out a professional photo session, prepare a detailed technical sheet and write a precise description. We don't inflate qualities or conceal limitations. Serious buyers value transparency.

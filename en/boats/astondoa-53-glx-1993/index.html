Description

Astondoa 53 GLX, fiberglass cruiser monohull with 15.85m LOA, 4.72m beam and 2.45m draft. Displacement 30,270 kg.

Three cabins, full galley, two heads. Double crew quarters. Detroit 2 × 550 HP engines with generator. 850 engine hours.

Good overall condition, maintenance up to date. Cruising speed 27 knots, max speed 32 knots.

Equipped with radar, autopilot, sounder, VHF, bow thruster, electric controls, GPS plotter, electric windlass, hydraulic gangway, tender with outboard motor, fly cover.

Description

Spanish-built Navalia 60, year 2006. Cruiser with 18m LOA and fiberglass hull.

Four cabins, two heads. Caterpillar engines in very good condition with cruising speed of 20 knots and max speed of 30 knots. Electric windlass.

Teak cockpit and side decks. Equipped with joystick, hydraulic gangway, cockpit shower, tender, cockpit cushions and table. Electronics with radar, autopilot, GPS plotter, sounder, VHF, radar detector.

Surveyed and ready for extended holidays.

Description

Finnyacht 35, solidly built Nordic sailboat. 10.44m LOA, 3.12m beam, 1.69m draft. Full keel.

Large stern cockpit. Cruising speed 7-8 knots, great range of 300 nautical miles. Ideal for liveaboard.

Semi-new Vetus 67 HP engine with only 52 hours. Completely clean fuel tanks, stainless steel engine beds.

Sails: roller furling mainsail and genoa. Solar panel. Equipped with GPS plotter, VHF, compass, radio, flat screen TV. Oven, fridge, marine WC. Inverter, shore power, battery charger.

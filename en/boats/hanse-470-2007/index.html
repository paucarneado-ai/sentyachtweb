Description

Hanse 470, fiberglass monohull sailing yacht with 14.30m LOA. Leading German shipyard.

Three cabins, two heads. Interior Volvo engine with 101 HP, sail drive transmission. Electric windlass. 12V electrical system.

Sails: roller furling mainsail and genoa. Teak cockpit and side decks. Equipped with cockpit shower, davits, radar reflector, life raft, cockpit cushions and table, bathing ladder.

Full electronics: radar, autopilot, navigation center, GPS plotter, sounder, log/speed, radar detector, wind instruments, VHF.

Designed for quality and style sailing.

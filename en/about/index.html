About Sent-Yacht

Experts and specialists in yachts and large vessels. At Sent-Yacht, led by Jordi Sentíes, you will find transparency, professionalism and nautical knowledge — essential for a complete experience when considering the purchase or sale of a boat.

At Sent-Yacht we offer, with full guarantees, valuation services, purchase guidance, sale of selected vessels, maintenance for your yacht or boat, and — above all — personalised, professional attention.

Everything you could wish for in nautical services, from the hand of a specialist in large yachts, with extensive experience, quality and fair pricing.

« Al pot petit hi ha la bona confitura »

"In the small jar you find the finest preserve": with this popular Catalan expression, we can simply describe what Sent-Yacht is and what you will find here.

Jordi Sentíes

Jordi Sentíes is the driving force behind Sent-Yacht. He began his journey in the nautical world at Talleres Nautic in January 1976. He later acquired Ibermar Marina S.A., his first business venture, while working as a broker at Marina Masnou. In 1982 he joined Astilleros Astondoa, becoming International Commercial Director and creating Sent-Yacht, which included its own boatyard.

His deep knowledge of large vessels and decades-long ties with the most prestigious yacht builders — Astondoa, Pershing, Antago, Ferretti and Maiora, among others — set him apart. He served as president of FADIN (Spanish Federation of Nautical Industries, Commerce and Services) and ADIN (Catalan Association of Nautical Industries, Commerce and Services) for 8 years each.

At Sent-Yacht, based at Port Esportiu Masnou (also operating directly in Barcelona and other ports along the Catalan coast), you will find rigour, confidence and trust, with a personal touch.

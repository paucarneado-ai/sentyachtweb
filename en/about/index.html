El Masnou, Maresme Coast

Boat brokerage with criteria

SentYacht is a yacht brokerage based in El Masnou, backed by 50 years of experience in the nautical industry. We are not a listings portal or an international group. We work with a limited number of boats, selected with care, to offer direct treatment, a controlled sales process and real knowledge of what we sell.

Behind SentYacht

Jordi Senties

Jordi Senties has been in the nautical industry since January 1976. He started at Talleres Nautic, went on to acquire his first company — Ibermar Marina S.A. — and worked as a broker at Marina Masnou. In 1982 he joined Astilleros Astondoa, one of Spain's most prestigious shipyards, where he became International Commercial Director. It was during that period that he created Sent-Yacht, which included its own boatyard.

His knowledge of the sector comes from having lived it from the inside: workshop, boatyard, shipyard, commercial directorship and direct sales. He has worked with builders such as Astondoa, Pershing, Antago, Ferretti and Maiora. He is not a middleman who consults catalogues; he knows the vessels, their builders and their market through direct experience.

Jordi served as president of FADIN (the Spanish Federation of Nautical Industries, Commerce and Services) and ADIN (the Catalan Association of Nautical Industries, Commerce and Services) for eight years each. Today he manages every operation personally from El Masnou. One phone number, one contact, every step of the way.

Why a limited number of boats

There is a Catalan saying: al pot petit hi ha la bona confitura — in a small pot you find the best jam. We could manage more boats. We choose not to. A limited catalogue means more time per vessel: a well-founded valuation, careful photography, an honest description, and real follow-up on every viewing and every offer. For the owner, that means a better-managed sale. For the buyer, information you can trust.

El Masnou and the western Mediterranean

Our base is at Port Esportiu El Masnou, on the Maresme coast, 20 minutes north of Barcelona. It is a port that combines good connections with reasonable costs and a professional environment away from the congestion of Port Olímpic. From here we operate across Barcelona, the Maresme, the Costa Brava and the Balearic Islands. Jordi knows these ports, their nautical communities and their particularities because he has been working in them for decades.

How we work

With buyers

Every boat we present has verified documentation, a complete technical sheet and a precise description. We do not inflate qualities or conceal limitations. If a boat does not match what you are looking for, we tell you. And if we know of a better fit, we will recommend it — whether it is in our portfolio or not.

With owners

We manage the full process: valuation grounded in real market data, professional presentation, targeted marketing, buyer screening, viewings, negotiation and closing. The owner has a single point of contact from start to finish — someone who knows the vessel and can defend it with technical arguments.

How we understand brokerage

Transparency

Real condition, maintenance history, limitations included. No calculated omissions.

Selection criteria

We only manage boats we can back with technical knowledge and sell with honesty.

Confidentiality

Discreet sale if needed: no listing, no unnecessary exposure, access limited to qualified buyers.

Direct service

The same person who values the boat is the one who presents it, negotiates and closes. No departments, no rotation.

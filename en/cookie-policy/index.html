Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites store on the user's device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when visited. They serve to remember information about the visit, such as preferred language, configuration options, and other preferences, facilitating navigation and making the website more useful.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1. Technical Cookies (Necessary)

These allow navigation through the Website and use of its various options and services. These cookies are essential for the website to function and do not require user consent.

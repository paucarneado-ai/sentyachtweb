Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 on Personal Data Protection (LOPD-GDD), we inform you that personal data you provide will be processed by:

  • Controller: SentYacht
  • Address: Port Esportiu, Local 49, El Masnou, 08320, Barcelona, Spain
  • Email: info@sentyacht.com
  • Phone: +34 609 865 215

2. Purpose of Processing

Personal data collected through Website forms will be processed for the following purposes:

  • Managing enquiries and information requests from users about boats.
  • Sending commercial information related to our boat brokerage services, with prior user authorisation.
  • Maintaining the commercial relationship and providing requested services.
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing your personal data is:

  • Consent: by submitting a contact form or information request, the user expressly consents to the processing of their data for the stated purpose.
  • Contract performance: when processing is necessary for the provision of a service requested by the user.
  • Legitimate interest: for the improvement of our services and fraud prevention.
  • Legal obligation: for compliance with applicable legal obligations.

4. Personal Data Collected

Personal data we may collect through our Website includes:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User message or enquiry
  • Browsing data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)

5. Retention Period

Personal data will be retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected and to determine any liabilities arising from that purpose. Data will be retained in accordance with legally established timeframes.

6. Data Recipients

Personal data will not be disclosed to third parties, except where required by law. Where necessary, data may be communicated to:

  • Competent public authorities, in cases provided by law.
  • Service providers acting as data processors, with whom appropriate data processing agreements have been entered into.

7. Data Subject Rights

You may exercise the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access: to know whether your personal data is being processed and to obtain a copy.
  • Right of rectification: to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right of erasure: to request deletion of your data when no longer necessary.
  • Right to object: to object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right of restriction: to request restriction of processing of your data.
  • Right of portability: to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, please contact info@sentyacht.com with a copy of your identification document.

You also have the right to file a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (www.aepd.es) if you consider that the processing of your data does not comply with current regulations.

8. Security Measures

SentYacht has adopted the necessary technical and organisational measures to ensure the security of personal data and prevent its alteration, loss, unauthorised processing, or access, taking into account the state of technology, the nature of the data stored, and the risks to which it is exposed.

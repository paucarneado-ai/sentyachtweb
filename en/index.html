Since 1976

Not every boat should be sold the same way.

And not every broker operates at the same level.

When an owner decides to sell a boat of real value, they need more than exposure. They need judgment, positioning, market knowledge, and a professional representation capable of protecting both the vessel and the perception of its worth.

At SentYacht we work from that philosophy. We offer yacht brokerage to owners who expect a serious, discreet and carefully managed process, with direct attention and a commercial sense built on decades of real experience in the sector.

The career of Jordi Sentíes, which began in 1976, brings a solid foundation of knowledge, relationships and understanding of the Mediterranean market. He has worked with shipyards such as Astondoa, Pershing, Antago, Ferretti and Maiora — not as a middleman, but from within, as International Commercial Director of Astondoa for years.

That experience, combined with a personal and rigorous approach, allows us to accompany every operation with the professionalism a quality vessel demands. From El Masnou, with operational presence across Barcelona, the Maresme, the Costa Brava and the Balearic Islands.

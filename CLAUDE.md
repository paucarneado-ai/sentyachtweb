# CLAUDE.md

You are working on the SentYacht website.

This is a premium yacht brokerage website build.
Your job is not to produce generic luxury visuals.
Your job is to build a credible, commercially sharp, SEO-aware, high-end brokerage website.

The target outcome is a site that feels:

* premium
* sober
* Mediterranean
* commercially literate
* selective
* visually refined
* trustworthy for both buyers and sellers

The site must not feel like:

* a generic boat classifieds portal
* a fake global superyacht conglomerate
* a startup SaaS landing page
* a lifestyle magazine with weak commercial intent
* a bloated design exercise

---

## 1. PRIORITY ORDER

When making tradeoffs, follow this order:

1. Truthfulness to the real SentYacht positioning
2. Coherent information architecture
3. Strong buy/sell commercial flow
4. Seller capture strength
5. Boat detail quality
6. Premium visual quality
7. SEO structure
8. Motion / polish
9. Nice-to-have embellishments

If something looks impressive but weakens truthfulness or clarity, reject it.
If something adds complexity but weakens coherence, reject it.

---

## 2. CORE POSITIONING

The website must communicate within seconds:

1. SentYacht brokers selected boats seriously.
2. SentYacht helps owners sell with discretion and criteria.
3. SentYacht operates with direct treatment and Mediterranean focus.

The brand should feel expert and selective, not inflated.
Do not write fake "world-leading" or "global leader" language.
Do not imply services, scale, or international reach that are not clearly supported.

SentYacht base/location nuance matters:

* the business base is El Masnou
* Barcelona may be relevant for some boats or moorings
* do not collapse El Masnou into Barcelona as if they were the same thing

---

## 3. WEBSITE STRATEGY

This website has two primary funnels:

### Funnel A: Buyer

The user wants to browse available boats, understand the offer, inspect detail pages, and enquire.

### Funnel B: Seller

The user is considering selling a boat and wants confidence, process clarity, valuation guidance, and discreet contact.

Do not build the site as if buyer flow is the only important flow.
Seller capture is a first-class function.

---

## 4. REQUIRED V1 PAGES

Build a sharp V1 first.
Do not widen scope early.

Required pages:

* Home
* Boats for Sale / Listings index
* Boat Detail template
* Sell Your Boat

* Sold Boats
* About SentYacht
* Contact
* Location pages:

  * El Masnou
  * Barcelona

  * Baleares

Optional only if V1 is already strong:

* Buying Guide
* Insights / Editorial index

Do not start V2-style expansion until V1 is coherent, polished, and believable.

---

## 5. HOME PAGE RULES

The home page must clearly support both buying and selling.

Required order of sections:

1. Hero
2. Buy / Sell split block
3. Featured boats
4. Trust / credibility strip
5. Seller capture block
6. Boat category block
7. Location / geography block
8. Useful editorial block if strong enough
9. Final CTA
10. Footer

### Hero rules

* use one strong image or very restrained motion
* short headline
* short supporting line
* maximum two CTAs
* preferred CTA pair:

  * View Boats
  * Request Confidential Valuation

Do not use vague fantasy-luxury copy.
Emotion should come from visual quality and composition, not from overblown slogans.

### Buy / Sell split

Must be clear and visible high on the page.
Each side should have:

* a short value proposition
* one supporting sentence
* one CTA

Do not bury seller flow.

---

## 6. NAVIGATION RULES

The main navigation must stay disciplined.

Preferred top-level structure:

* Buy
* Sell
* Boats
* Valuation
* About
* Contact

Optional:

* language switcher

The navigation may expose secondary routes such as:

* boats by type
* sold boats
* selling process
* location pages
* buying guide

Do not create a huge corporate mega-menu with services that do not truly exist.
Do not imitate global brokerage groups mechanically.

---

## 7. LISTINGS RULES

The listings experience must feel premium but usable.
It must not feel like marketplace clutter.

### Listings page must include:

* title and short intro
* filters
* sort control
* results grid
* pagination or load more
* discreet seller CTA
* useful SEO-supporting copy below results

### Minimum filters:

* boat type
* length
* price
* year
* location

Do not overbuild filters if the inventory does not justify it.
Precision matters more than apparent sophistication.

### Listing card must show:

* main image
* boat name
* builder/model or type
* year / refit if relevant
* length
* cabins
* price or price on request
* location
* CTA: View Details

Cards must be visually clean.
Do not overload metadata.

---

## 8. BOAT DETAIL PAGE RULES

Boat detail pages are critical.
They must combine desire, facts, and enquiry.

Required structure:

1. Hero gallery
2. Quick summary bar
3. Key specifications block
4. Highlights block
5. Commercial description
6. Refit / condition / maintenance block if relevant
7. Extended gallery
8. Enquiry block
9. Related boats

### Summary/specs must prioritize facts:

* builder / model
* year
* refit year if applicable
* length
* cabins
* location
* price
* engines
* other relevant specs if available

### Highlights rules

Use specific, real highlights.
Do not use fluff.
Examples of acceptable highlights:

* major refit completed
* hydraulic swim platform
* updated navigation electronics
* flybridge jacuzzi
* new interior upholstery
* serviced generators

### Copy rules

Facts over adjectives.
Precision over salesy noise.
If technical or presentation upgrades exist, foreground them clearly.

---

## 9. SELLER FUNNEL RULES

Seller capture is not secondary.
Treat it as a major business function.

### Sell Your Boat page must include:

* strong intro
* why sell with SentYacht
* process in 4 to 6 steps
* how valuation works
* presentation/marketing logic
* discretion/confidentiality messaging
* FAQ
* form / CTA

### Confidential Valuation page must include:

* why request a valuation
* what affects value
* what information is needed
* discreet form
* confidentiality reassurance

Do not claim fake instant precision.
Frame valuation honestly as a professional estimate range based on:

* type
* make/model
* year
* length
* location
* condition
* maintenance/refit history
* market comparables

---

## 10. SOLD BOATS RULES

Build a sold boats page even if initial volume is limited.
It supports:

* proof of commercial activity
* catalog depth
* seller confidence
* internal linking

Each sold context should support a seller CTA such as:
"Thinking of selling your boat? Request a confidential valuation."

Required pages:

* El Masnou

## 12. SEO RULES

This site must have intentional SEO architecture.
Not just page titles.

Core hubs to support:

* /boats-for-sale
* /motor-yachts-for-sale
* /sailing-yachts-for-sale
* /catamarans-for-sale
* /boats-for-sale-barcelona
* /boats-for-sale-baleares
* /sell-your-boat
* /confidential-valuation
* /sold-boats

Optional hubs only if content/inventory justify them:

* by length range
* by brand
* deeper regional pages
* buying guide content

### SEO principles

* avoid thin duplicate pages
* avoid useless filter-index bloat
* use strong internal linking
* ensure good metadata per page
* optimize images seriously
* performance matters

Do not create fake content depth.
Depth is only good if it is commercially and editorially real.

---

## 13. VISUAL DIRECTION

The visual system should feel:

* premium
* spacious
* restrained
* calm
* Mediterranean
* elegant but usable

Avoid:

* tech-startup gradients
* dark glossy gimmicks everywhere
* fake-luxury gold overload
* busy motion-heavy layouts
* sterile classifieds styling

### Visual direction guidance

* strong photography
* controlled palette
* refined typography hierarchy
* generous spacing
* disciplined cards
* sober buttons

The site should feel more like a serious premium brokerage than an ad-tech landing page.

---

## 14. MOTION RULES

Motion must serve quality perception, not theatre.

Allowed:

* subtle fades
* short hover transitions
* refined gallery transitions
* calm menu reveal
* restrained parallax only if clearly beneficial

Avoid:

* long autoplay sliders as a storytelling crutch
* noisy scroll gimmicks
* exaggerated parallax
* decorative animation that slows comprehension

If motion distracts from the boat, the CTA, or the content hierarchy, it is bad motion.

---

## 15. COPY RULES

The tone must feel:

* direct
* discreet
* calm
* commercially literate
* premium but grounded

The tone must not feel:

* fake luxury brochure copy
* cheap lead-gen spam
* over-SEO text stuffing
* vague startup language

### Writing rules

* facts over flourish
* precision over ornament
* confidence without exaggeration
* short paragraphs
* no empty adjectives stacking

Do not overclaim experience, network, or reach.
If proof is not available, do not imply it.

## 17. IMPLEMENTATION DISCIPLINE

Work in narrow, coherent increments.
Do not widen scope casually.

Preferred execution order:

1. Architecture / routes / data shape
2. Home page core
3. Listings index
4. Boat detail template
5. Seller pages
6. About / Contact / Location pages
7. SEO metadata and internal linking
8. Polish and motion refinement

If there is a choice between "more impressive" and "more believable," choose believable.
If there is a choice between "more complex" and "more coherent," choose coherent.
If there is a choice between "more global" and "more truthful to SentYacht," choose truthful.

---

## 18. ACCEPTANCE TEST

The work is only acceptable if:

* the home clearly communicates buy + sell within seconds
* the site feels premium without pretending to be a multinational group
* listings feel curated and clear
* boat detail pages are commercially strong and information-rich
* seller capture is visible and serious
* location pages support real Mediterranean positioning
* SEO structure is intentional
* the visual language is controlled and high-end
* CTAs are consistent and restrained
* the whole site feels coherent

If the result is merely pretty but commercially weak, it fails.
If the result is luxurious but inflated and untruthful, it fails.
If the result is functional but visually cheap, it fails.

Build something tighter, truer, and more commercially intelligent than a generic benchmark clone.

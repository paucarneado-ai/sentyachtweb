## Phase 3 — Sustained Cadence (Month 2+)

### Format mix per week
- **Tuesday**: alternating Boat of the Week (reel or carousel) / Seller Education
- **Friday**: alternating Jordi authority / Mediterranean expertise / Broker reel

### Format priorities
- **Reels for discovery** (new page needs reach — carousels don't get it organically)
- **Carousels for engagement** (1.5-2x higher engagement, reward swipe depth)
- **Authentic phone-recorded video** beats polished studio content by 44% on CTR
- **Link posts are DEAD** — never put a link in the post body (FB demotes unverified pages to ~2 links/month)

### Rebalanced Boat of the Week format
- 50% reels (boat reveal + Jordi voiceover)
- 50% carousels (spec deep-dives, refit before/after)

## Phase 8 — Compliance (DSA 2026 + Spanish law)

### Must have
- [ ] **Consent Mode v2** on landing page before Pixel/CAPI fires
- [ ] **Document lawful basis + retention** for lead-form data (GDPR/LOPDGDD)
- [ ] **DPA with Meta Ireland** on file
- [ ] **Written testimonial consent forms** (not verbal) — GDPR requirement
- [ ] **Photo rights clause** in brokerage contracts (pre-sale and post-sale)
- [ ] **LSSI-CE compliance**: company name, CIF, registered address in About section
- [ ] **2FA on all admin accounts**

### Content rules
- No "best broker" / "número 1" claims (Spanish Ley de Competencia Desleal)
- No exact prices for boats above 500K EUR ("Precio a consultar")
- Prices under 100K EUR: show in caption
- Prices 100K-500K EUR: show in caption, emphasize condition
- No engagement bait: "Tag a friend", "Comment YES", "Vote in comments", reaction-fishing
- No watermarked reposts from TikTok/Instagram (triggers suppression)
- Response SLA: aim <2 hours during business hours (73% of users buy from a competitor if no response)

## Phase 9 — Metrics & Review

### Monthly review (every 30 days)

**Primary KPIs:**
1. Qualified enquiries sourced to Facebook/Instagram (revenue metric)
2. Saves per post (strongest luxury intent proxy)
3. DMs/Messenger conversations initiated
4. New follower quality (demographic check vs buyer persona)
5. Retargeting pool size
6. Paid CPL trend (raw + qualified)
7. Top 3 posts by saves and DMs

**Diagnostic KPIs:**
- Reach per post
- Engagement rate (compared to month-over-month, not global benchmarks)
- Video: **ThruPlay, 3-second views, avg watch time** (10s view metric deprecated Jan 26, 2026)

**Stop tracking:**
- Raw follower count as a primary KPI
- Likes/reactions
- Impressions
- Engagement rate in isolation

## Biggest Risks

### Risk 1: Content collapse by month 4
Jordi too busy, cadence slips, page goes quiet, Meta Ads quality score craters, credibility surface becomes liability. **A dead page is worse than no page.**

**Mitigation:**
- 2 posts/week ceiling (not 3)
- Batch content monthly (not weekly)
- Pre-record Jordi interview as content reservoir (Plan B)
- Kill switch committed in writing so it's not an emotional decision in month 6

### Risk 2: Jordi refuses camera
Listed as Plan B/C above.

### Risk 3: Lead quality is low
Raw CPL looks good but qualified CPL is garbage. Pre-qualify forms with budget/timeline fields.

### Risk 4: Instagram gets neglected
Cross-post strategy only — don't promise more.
